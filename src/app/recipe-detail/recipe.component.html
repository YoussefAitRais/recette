Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meat.

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter/oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the eggs and let cook for 1â€“2 mins until edges set.
  4. Add fillings: Once edges are set, add fillings on one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Carefully fold, cook for another minute, then serve.
